Established in 1989
Harry Price established Chemfreight back in 1989 as the first specialist 3PL provider of Hazardous storage for the packaged market.
We are specialists
We specialise in providing a complete 3PL solution that takes care of the storage, handling and transport of all classes of Hazardous and also non-Hazardous products.

our services
With nearly 30 years of specialising in Hazardous storage and Dangerous Goods transport, Chemfreight is New Zealand’s most experienced and largest third party logistics company that specialises in Hazardous.
We specialise in the storage of Hazardous Substances (Classes 1 – 9) and also Hazardous and non-Hazardous Food Grade products and the distribution of Dangerous Goods.
Chemfreight has three state of the art Hazardous storage facilities in Auckland and a large multi-Hazardous storage facility servicing the South Island located in Christchurch.
We provide the complete solution of Hazardous Substance storage, chemical toll blending and Dangerous Goods transport and we are also 100% New Zealand family owned and operated.
We also have a chemical toll blending and repackaging facility for both Hazardous and non-Hazardous Substances.
All our facilities are state of the art and we invite you to take a tour of any of our four locations across Auckland and Christchurch.
