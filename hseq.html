Our HSEQ philosophy
The Chemfreight philosophy is that Health, Safety, Environment and Quality (HSEQ) are integral components of our business and that HSEQ objectives are ranked equally with the Chemfreight service and corporate objectives (including financial).
Total HSEQ integration
At Chemfreight, HSEQ is integrated with all functions of the business. Whilst the leadership has ultimate accountability, responsibility for HSEQ is with everyone who works, visits, or has business at Chemfreight sites.
Fully Certified facilities
Chemfreight’s facilities in Auckland and Christchurch have been designated Major Hazard Facilities under the new legislation. Chemfreight is currently writing Safety Cases for approval by WorkSafe.
Chemfreight is committed to providing and constantly improving on a healthy and safe environment for its workers and visitors by complying with or exceeding the requirements of the Health and Safety at Work Act 2015 and all other relevant health, safety and environmental legislation.
As part of Chemfreight’s ongoing compliance programme, Chemfreight is fully compliant with HSNO requirements including; Location Test Certificates, Approved Handlers and HSNO compliant warehouses.
As a commitment to Quality and the Environment, Chemfreight is ISO 9001:2015 certified and is currently working towards ISO 14001 approval.
Improvement programme
As part of Chemfreight’s continuing improvement programme, Chemfreight is a member of the New Zealand Institute of Hazardous Substance Management and Responsible Care New Zealand.
